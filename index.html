Welcome

This Time Zone Meeting Aid App let's you calculate what the date and time
will be for another meeting participant, based on your location, their location,
and when you want to have the meeting.


INSTRUCTIONS FOR USE

  1. Select the date and time when you would like to have the meeting.

  2. Find your location on the map. Zoom in with the scroll wheel
    or zoom buttons. Pan around the map by holding down the
    scroll wheel, or the left mouse button.

  3. Press Log Coordinates and then left-click the mouse on
    your location on the map.

  4. Find the location of the other participant on the map.

  5. Press Log Coordinates for the particpant and then
    left-click the mouse on their location on the map.

  6. Press the Results button to see their date and time.

  7. Press the Save button to download an image of the app.

If you need to clear any of the coordinates,
use the relevant Clear Coordinates button.
